Will I be charged for customs & import duties?

Unfortunately, if you are an International customer then you will be charged for customs and import taxes. This will depend on the weight of your parcel and the country the parcel has been shipped to. Beauty Works is not responsible for any customs or import costs.

What if I am not in at the time of delivery?

As our couriers are operating contact free delivery, you will need to be present to obtain your parcel from our couriers at a safe distance. The courier will leave your parcel with you safely and will take an image to confirm safe receipt. If you are not at the delivery address at the time of delivery, a second attempt will be made the next day as well as a final third attempt. If the parcel has still not been delivered then it will be returned to the sender. If our courier deems it safe to do so, your parcel may be left with a neighbour.

Our couriers cannot leave any of our parcels in a “safe place” and any instruction between you and the courier, Beauty Works will not held responsible in the event of loss or damage.

What should I do if my order does not arrive?

If your order does not arrive we advise checking with neighbours or if the item had been left in a safe place. A calling card will be left by the courier advising of the parcels location. If you are still unable to retrieve your parcel please contact us within 24 hours and we can contact the courier to raise an investigation.

With all investigations and claims we process, we must make you aware that we record the claim via Action Fraud UK which is a Police database. With the increase of online fraud, we want to protect our genuine customers as well as monitor the fraudulent claims. Please note this is not in any way an indication that you are fraudulent customer, we want to reassure you that we investigate thoroughly and take these matters seriously. Please see their website for any further information you may require: https://www.actionfraud.police.uk/

Coronavirus (COVID-19) – Have any precautions been put in place?

Beauty Works is operating business as usual and we are working closely with couriers to ensure all deliveries arrive on time. The wellbeing of our customers is a top priority and all necessary precautions are being taken to ensure the safety of both customers and couriers during this time, including contact-free deliveries. You will not be required to sign for your parcel, our couriers will complete this on your behalf.
