Pricing & Costing Analyst

A newly created role in a fast-paced beauty business with strong forecasts and pipeline for 2021. Sitting within the finance team whilst working closely with the buying and marketing department to provide valuable insights into business. This role will be focused on providing support to facilitate exciting growth plans and improve current processes.....

Main duties consist of:

  • Manage new product line documents and prepare pricing and set company margin
  • Set all pricing for new lines per season
  • Audit all pricelists for all tiers
  • Ensuring all prices are correctly documented and consistent within all sales channels
  • Work with ecommerce team and other departments to set and check margin for promotions online or for retailers
  • Review sales details by week and highlight trends to management per SKU in the absence of the purchasing manager
  • Report trends back to purchasing buyer and share weekly report to management in the absence of the purchasing manager
  • Producing flash reports for senior management in the absence of the purchasing manager
  • Any other adhoc projects or tasks required from the finance or buying departments

Experience

  • AAT qualification essential
  • 2 years prior experience required
  • NetSuite system experience desirable
  • Excel knowledge essential

Location

South Manchester

Ecommerce Assistant


What will the role include?

  • Uploading and updating products including writing descriptions and uploading imagery.
  • Merchandising & upselling products to ensure best sellers and key products are highlighted.
  • Writing SEO descriptions and producing blog content.....
  • Uploading price changes.
  • Making & testing promo codes.
  • Support the execution of onsite content, campaigns and trading activity.
  • Proof-reading marketing emails & auditing the website on a daily basis.
  • General admin duties and assisting the eCommerce and Marketing teams.
  • Build effective working relationships and actively participate in internal meetings.

What will your responsibilities be?

  • Trading
    • Working closely with Ecommerce Manager & wider teams to ensure product launches happen on time and products are assigned to all appropriate listing pages.
    • Maximise cross selling opportunities through product recommendations, continual review of performance and placement.
    • Ensuring all promotional/sale activity is set up accurately and tested ahead of launch working alongside Ecommerce executive.
  • Onsite Content
    • Monitoring performance of onsite content and proposing changes to maximise revenue and profit.
    • Working closely with Marketing, Buying and Ecommerce Manager to influence the development/testing roadmap.
    • Ensure that each item on sale is fully optimized for copy and imagery for both the customer and for SEO.
    • Regular review of competitor activity to propose UX improvements/tests.
    • Support with UAT of new onsite features/capabilities as and when required.
  • Other
    • Troubleshooting website/pricing/customer issues and liaising with appropriate teams to resolve issues and provide feedback in a timely manner.
    • Support wider team where appropriate, e.g. channel landing page testing.
    • Keep up to date with industry best practices and new platform features to ensure we’re getting the most from the information/tools available.

What are we looking for?

  • Min 1 years ecommerce experience in Retail or similar B2C industry.
  • A strong commercial mindset and customer first approach.
  • Excellent data and analytics skills, with confident use of Excel to manipulate data into meaningful insight.
  • Highly organised and methodical with ability to prioritise workload effectively.
  • Excellent writing skills with an understanding of SEO content.
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced business.
  • A passion for fashion/retail and detail but always seeks continuous improvement.
  • Proactive and positive approach to problem solving - a can do attitude.
  • Team player with excellent communication skills both internally and externally.
