PRICING & COSTING ANALYST

A newly created role in a fast-paced beauty business with strong forecasts and pipeline for 2021.....

Sitting within the finance team whilst working closely with the buying and marketing department to provide valuable insights into business. This role will be focused on providing support to facilitate exciting growth plans and improve current processes.

Main duties consist of:

  • Manage new product line documents and prepare pricing and set company margin
  • Set all pricing for new lines per season
  • Audit all pricelists for all tiers
  • Ensuring all prices are correctly documented and consistent within all sales channels
  • Work with ecommerce team and other departments to set and check margin for promotions online or for retailers
  • Review sales details by week and highlight trends to management per SKU in the absence of the purchasing manager
  • Report trends back to purchasing buyer and share weekly report to management in the absence of the purchasing manager
  • Producing flash reports for senior management in the absence of the purchasing manager
  • Any other adhoc projects or tasks required from the finance or buying departments

Experience

  • AAT qualification essential
  • 2 years prior experience required
  • NetSuite system experience desirable
  • Excel knowledge essential

CREDIT CONTROLLER

A varied role in a fast-paced beauty business, requiring strong reconciliation skills, sale’s ledger application knowledge and overall credit control experience. ....

Sitting within the finance team you will be working with another team member applying incoming payments against the business’s sales ledger, debt chasing accounts, whilst ensuring full audit reconciliations are done on the business’s key accounts.

This role will be focused on improving the businesses sales ledger position, whilst ensuring the full customer experience is given. Working alongside key account managers, the warehouse, and the sales team.

Main duties consist of:

  • Full audit reconciliation of the business’s key accounts, to ensure all invoices are paid within the payment terms agreed.
  • Processing debit notes and rectifying any pricing or quantity queries raised in a timely manner.
  • Accountability of all incoming payments to the business, ensuring they are allocated correctly to the sales ledger where appropriate.
  • Reconciling payments for the cash sales of the business to the funds received in the bank.
  • Debt chasing of key accounts and smaller customers who hold terms with the business.
  • Reconciling open invoices in the ERP system
  • Producing weekly and monthly reports
  • Calculating related KPI’s and understand what influences these and how they can be improved.
  • Setting up of new customers within the ERP system
  • Monitoring the credit limits of existing customers
  • Taking ownership for all functions relating to credit control
  • Carry out adhoc finance related tasks.

Experience

  • Qualification in credit and collections through CICM desirable
  • AAT qualification desirable
  • 5 years minimum prior experience required.
  • NetSuite system experience desirable
  • Excel knowledge essential

Location

South Manchester
