What aftercare should I use in the sun?

During the summer and in hotter climates our hair is exposed to more UV light which can affect the colour of hair. Too much sun exposure, heat styling or pollution can cause hair to feel and look dry, damaged and dull. Using the correct shampoo and conditioner will protect the hair. Beauty Works After Sun Deep Cleanse Shampoo, is created specifically for post-sun exposure. Formulated to eliminate product build-up of sand, salt and sun cream from the scalp it also acts as a deep cleanse for pollution particles, dirt and excess oils. Follow with the Beauty Works conditioner which softens and brings back the shine to your hair extensions.

How do you protect hair from colour fade?

Beauty Works After Sun Colour Lockdown Shampoo prevents colour fade and keeps hair colour vibrant. Infused with Argan Oil and Macadamia Oil, this super-nourishing shampoo keeps your hair healthy and hydrated while the added UV defense reverses signs of damage from sun exposure. Use before you go in the sun and on colour treated hair to keep your hair vibrant.

When should I use a Clarifying Shampoo?

Think of clarifying shampoos as shampoos to cut through product build-up and oils to leave your hair squeaky clean. There are lots of reasons why hair can look sad and lifeless, too much heat styling and even residue from normal shampoos and conditioners, all of which can cling to hair and scalp, making your freshly-washed hair seem heavy. Use a clarifying shampoo to cleanse hair monthly if you have normal hair, or weekly if you have greasy hair.

Do your products contain Sulphates?

Yes, certain lines contain sulphates, sulphates are used in shampoo. The purpose of these sulphates is to create a lathering effect to remove oil and dirt from your hair

Are Sulphates bad for me?

This will be determined by your hair type, you may want to avoid sulphates if you have dry or fine hair. These hair types are more fragile, and the effects of sulphate shampoo can strip out too much of the natural oils needed to keep your strands healthy.

BEAUTY WORKS HAIR SOURCING

Where is Beauty Works hair extensions from?

Beauty Works source raw human hair extensions from all over the world. Beauty Works hair extensions is part of the largest manufacturer of human hair in the world and work with factories both in India and China to manufacture our production line. This allows us to deliver the highest quality hair at the best price possible. Our partner’s source our raw hair from Malaysia, Vietnam, Mongolia, India and China, depending on the product range this is than manufactured to our specifications before it arrives at Beauty Works.

Is Beauty Works hair extensions ethically sourced?

We source our hair straight from verified distributors, who depend on their long-standing reputation for ethical sourcing and quality hair. Human hair is donated, sold, or exchanged fairly and ethically in the region its sourced. Selling hair is a supplementary source of income for women, which does not require any labour.

How is the hair sourced?

Our factory partners source hair from regions all over the world which are renowned for women donating healthy, strong and long hair lengths. Hair is never chemically treated and grows to lengths up to 32”. The majority of human hair used in extensions comes from India and Asia. Religious believes in Hindu religion believe sacrificing hair is a sign of devotion. Daily pilgrimages to temples such as the Venkateswara Temple in Tirumala, India, where they shave/tonsure their heads in a ritual of devotion. In Asian communities’ women donate their ponytails for a fair value, the hair is willingly donated and cut at the ponytail, leaving the donor with a bob length hair style. The ponytail bundles are than sorted into length and size and exported to our factories where the production of hair extensions starts.

Beauty Works Factory

With over 30 years in the hair extension industry, our factory partners are globally recognised as the leaders in human hair development. The team consists of over 1000 employees whom are skilled at sewing, colouring and crafting to produce quality hair extensions to Beauty Works specifications. From sourcing materials to arranging production, our factory partners strive for excellence every step of the way. Our hair is sourced directly from verified distributors who have a long-standing reputation for ethically sourcing quality human hair. All hair is sold or exchanged freely and fairly.

How does Beauty Works audit their supply chain?

Beauty Works hair suppliers are required to abide by our Supplier Code of Conduct. We do not condone any unethical treatment of workers or donors in our supply chain. Included in our “Supplier Code of Conduct” are Labor Standards and Ethical Hair Sourcing provisions. Our Ethical Hair Sourcing policy requires our factories to ensure fair compensation for hair purchases from donors/suppliers and prohibits coercion in collection of hair. All suppliers have signed agreements to abide by our Supplier Code of Conduct. We confirm compliance through detailed questionnaires, regular factory visits, and Social Compliance Audits conducted by third-party auditors. Because of these standards, we have high confidence that our hair products are ethically sourced and produced.
